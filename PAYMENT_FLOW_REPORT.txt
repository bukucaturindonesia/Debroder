CUSTOMER PAYMENT INSTRUCTIONS:
- /payment/[token] displays order number, customer, item summary, totals,
  verified amount, balance, requirement, expiry, and dynamic active methods.
- Account/QRIS details, copy actions, method instructions, and a clear warning
  explain that proof upload is not final payment confirmation.

PAYMENT SETTINGS:
- payment_method_settings is the central source for bank transfer, QRIS, and
  e-wallet destinations, owner/account details, instructions, expiry, sort,
  and active state.
- Authorized Admins manage settings from /admin/payments.
- No bank account, QRIS, or financial destination is hardcoded or seeded.

PAYMENT PROOF UPLOAD:
- Customer submission remains token-protected, server-only, and idempotent.
- Files remain in private payment-proofs storage.
- Limits: PNG/JPG/PDF, maximum 5 MB, MIME allowlist, and signature validation.
- Deterministic idempotency paths plus database keys prevent duplicate payment
  records and make retry reuse the same order/payment.

ADMIN PAYMENT WORKSPACE:
- Displays the customer report, private proof, destination method, mutation
  amount/account/time/reference, internal notes, and five required checks.
- Outcomes: Verify Funds, Funds Not Found, Request Correction, and Reject.
- Customer-safe history exposes correction/funds-not-found/rejection reasons
  without internal notes or proof URLs.

BANK MUTATION CONFIRMATION / SOURCE OF TRUTH:
- Verification requires actual funds, destination account, amount, timestamp,
  unique bank reference, active order, pending payment, proof, and fresh row.
- Bank/provider settlement values are stored separately from reported values.
- Verified totals use verified_amount, not the customer's claimed amount.

UNDERPAYMENT / OVERPAYMENT:
- Settlement classification records partial, exact, overpayment,
  under_reported, or over_reported.
- Partial values continue to use existing payment requirement/balance logic.
- Overpayments remain visible in immutable payment history and can use the
  existing adjustment/refund approval flow.

DUPLICATE PAYMENT PROTECTION / IDEMPOTENCY:
- Public submissions reuse submission_idempotency_key.
- Verified bank references have a normalized partial unique index.
- Review RPC locks payment and order rows and rejects stale timestamps or
  already-reviewed payments.

AUTHORIZATION / AUDIT:
- Public clients cannot call submission/review/settings RPCs directly.
- Admin JWT, role gate, database permission checks, and RPC grants are enforced.
- Authenticated access to legacy verify/reject RPCs is revoked to prevent
  checklist bypass; trusted service-role composition is retained server-side.
- Settings changes and payment outcomes are written to existing audit/history
  domains. Payment activity remains append-only.
